I'm a huge Witcher fan and I've always wanted to find a mod that makes Geralt look like the lean, pale old man from the books. I'm not really a fan of CDPR's interpretation of Geralt — especially adding the scar, which wasn't in the books.


![Geralt](https://i.imgur.com/vydQG2m.png)

I waited and waited for the right mod, and after 4 years I got tired of waiting and decided to learn Witcher modding myself. Using WolvenKit, I created my own mod that adds the eyes, face, beard, scars, hairstyles, and much more — all based on the books.

Enjoy.
